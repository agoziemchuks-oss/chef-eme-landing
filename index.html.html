The One Thing Nobody Tells You

For all the romance and adventure of loving a Nigerian man, there's one truth that almost no one prepares you for: food is not just food in Nigerian culture. It is love, respect, and belonging — all served in one pot.

When you cook for a Nigerian man the way his culture taught him love should taste, you're not just feeding him. You're saying: "I see you. I honour where you come from. I belong here with you." That message goes deeper than any date night ever could.

But here's the challenge: Nigerian cuisine is rich, complex, and deeply layered. There are over 250 ethnic groups in Nigeria, each with their own dishes, spices, and traditions. Without a guide, it can feel overwhelming — even intimidating. Where do you start? What does he actually want to eat? Which dishes matter most? What are the spices, and where do you find them?
